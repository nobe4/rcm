Random Chord Machine

Hey, welcome to RCM.
As you can already see, it's generating music for you.
The notation is based on the tabular notation with the added S for silence. A chord with no modulation (maj, min, ...) is describing the root note alone.
The symbols are showing the rythm used for each chord, if you want to see the chord's composition, hit notes.

You can play along with the different parameters:

Play
Self explanatory.
Metro
Add a tick every quarter notes.
Reload
Reload the chords.
Notes
Display the notes forming the chord.
Equal
Change the rythm generation method.
simple
The list of available modulations.
120
The tempo against the quarter note.
16
The number of 16th notes you want.
80
The volume.
